The Nexus Fellowship is an artist residency designed to onboard creatives to the web3 ecosystem

For our inaugural cohort, we will host 15-20 fellows on a 3 week retreat that will serve as a space to connect with other creatives, and find a platform in the web3 ecosystem that is aligned with their creative pursuits.

Applications will be out mid-June for our Fall 2022 Retreat.
